About Paper Survival Game

Cards

Generic Properties

Top: Card Name ; Card Type

Name is the card itself while type is a group of cards that share the same properties

Middle: Card Image

The image like the name is for identifying the card

Bottom: Card Power

The power is a number that shows how the card affects, bigger the value, more affective it will be

Types

Action Cards

Related to the actions for the player who uses it, like teleporting or getting resources

Build Cards

Creates elements on the map which are owned by the player, like houses

Creature Cards

Applies effects on the map for everyone, like storms

Resource Cards

Resources extracted or gained by the players, which can be used by other cards or as points (end of the game)

Power

Power is a scale of how affective the card is, higher the number, higher the power

Cards with more power affect more than the less powerful ones

For example: ender power 1 can get less distance than a power 5 ender
